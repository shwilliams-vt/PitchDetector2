Our Tool - Prototype

You can toggle the tool on and off by creating 2 transients in short time (e.g. clapping twice). The status will be listed as ON or OFF.

When first turned on, you will see that the tool is listening for a pitch. Once it confidently detects one, a session will begin, and you will see the detected pitch of your whistling/humming.

When the tool loses confidence (e.g. you stop whistling/humming), it will go back to the listening stage and the session will end.

Please click continue when you feel comfortable understanding our pitch-based NVVI tool. You may ask your proctor for help or more information.

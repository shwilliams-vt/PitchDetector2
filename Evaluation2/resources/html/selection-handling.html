How Our Tool Handles Selection

To specify when you want to use our NVVI tool, we decided that transient detection will be a way to toggle the abledness of our tool. In short, transient detection means a detection of some abrupt and atypically loud impulse in the audio signal.

--l--l-- hearing

Our tool interprets TWO TRANSIENTS as a signal to toggle the tool on and off. For example, you can do this by clapping your hands twice consecutively near the microphone! Listen to the demo below.

How Our Tool Handles Selection

To specify when to activate our NVVI tool's pitch detection, we've decided that transient detection will be the method of choice. In short, transient detection means the detection of some abrupt and atypically loud impulse in the audio signal.

--l--l-- hearing

Our tool interprets TWO TRANSIENTS as a signal to toggle the tool on and off. For example, you can do this by clapping your hands twice consecutively near the microphone! Listen to the demo below.

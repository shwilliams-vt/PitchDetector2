Study Consent

You are invited to participate in a research study. This form includes information about the study and contact information if you have any questions.

WHAT SHOULD I KNOW?

If you decide to participate in this study, you will complete several surveys and tests. As part of the study, you will perform tests using a pitch-based NVVI prototype and computer mouse to control HTML sliders. You will also respond to a demographic questionnaire, post-test surveys (including a NASA Task-Load Index questionnaire), and a System Usability Scale (SUS) survey. You will be prompted to allow microphone access to run the study, as such audio data will be input to the NVVI tools. Data being collected in this study to analyze the effectiveness of this NVVI tool includes metrics from tasks, like time taken and sampled slider locations, and questionnaire results. The study should take approximately 30 minutes of your time. You should work alone in a quiet setting for the duration of the study to minimize noise interference. We do not anticipate any risks from completing this study. You can choose whether to be in this study or not. If you volunteer to be in this study, you may withdraw at any time without consequences of any kind. You may also refuse to answer any questions you donâ€™t want to answer and remain in the study. The investigator may withdraw you from this research if circumstances arise which warrant doing so.

CONFIDENTIALITY

We will do our best to protect the confidentiality of the information we gather from you, but we cannot guarantee 100% confidentiality. Your responses are anonymous, so no one can associate your answers back to you. Please do not include your name or other identifying information in your responses that can identify you.

WHO CAN I TALK TO?

If you have any questions or concerns about the research, please feel free to contact shwilliams@vt.edu. You are not waiving any legal claims, rights or remedies because of your participation in this research study. If you have questions regarding your rights as a research participant, contact the Virginia Tech HRPP Office at 540-231-3732 (irb@vt.edu).

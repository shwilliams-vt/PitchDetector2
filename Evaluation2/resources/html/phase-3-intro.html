Phase 3 - Introduction

Phase 3 of this study will examine the effectiveness of using our tool to control HTML sliders. You will control the tool by your preferred method of whistling/humming in this phase. You must pick only one to use for the entire phase.

You will be tasked with using our pitch-based NVVI tool to control an HTML slider. By raising and lowering the pitch of your your preferred method of whistling/humming, you can adjust the relative position of the slider. When done, turn the tool off to progress to the next test. You will complete a series of these tasks.

A short demo is provided below for you to get comfortable if you wish. You may ask a proctor for assistance at any time. Please click the continue button to proceed with Phase 3 of the study.

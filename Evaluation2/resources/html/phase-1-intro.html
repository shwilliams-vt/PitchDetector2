Phase 1 - Introduction

Phase 1 of this study will examine the effectiveness of using our tool to control HTML sliders. You will control the tool by humming in this phase.

You will be tasked with using our pitch-based NVVI tool described in the previous slide to control an HTML slider. By raising and lowering the pitch of your humming, you are able to adjust the relative position of the slider. You will complete a series of these tasks.

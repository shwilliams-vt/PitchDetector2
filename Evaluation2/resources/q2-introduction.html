Welcome to a study on NVVI (Non-verbal Vocal Interaction)

In this study, you will be assessed by using an NVVI tool to complete tasks. In short, you will be using the pitch of your voice, whistle, or hum, to control a parameter. The parameter, for example, may control an HTML slider, but just think of a "parameter" as some sort of one-dimensional spectrum that can either increase or decrease in value. For example, the pitch you produce by humming can be analyzed as a numeric (continuous) value in Hertz and can be further analyzed if it is increasing or decreasing. Please speak with your proctor if you need a greater explanation.

By clicking the continue button below, you are confirming your participation in the study and consent to your specific but personally unidentifiable data being used in this study to analyze general NVVI trends. You may now ask the proctor for an itemized list of collected information. You may leave the study now or at any time during the study if you do not wish to participate, and your data will be deleted. Your unique ID is presented to you in the top left-hand corner of the screen. Once you click continue, the study will begin.
